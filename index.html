About

Hi - I'm Niklas!

... and I'm currently living in Stuttgart, Germany. In 2022, I graduated with a Master's degree in Software Engineering at the University of Stuttgart, where I am currently a PhD candidate researching gamified intelligent tutoring systems in higher software engineering education. In 2025, I spent several months at the University of California, Irvine (USA) as part of an academic exchange. Besides, I am passionate about planning and managing software projects to fulfill customer needs, and I always push myself to achieve personal and professional goals. In my opinion, good teamwork is the key to excellent software engineering and great team development. Together, we can achieve great success.
